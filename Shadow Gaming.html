Daily Farming Guide Videos By Shadow Gaming™

Every morning at 11:00am PST I upload a Daily Farming Guide video. Which covers the Targeted Loot map for the day, Dark Zone Exclusives you can farm for, as well as named items, gear and weapons, exotics, vendor highlights for the week, and build and farming suggestions as well. Sometimes I am unable to make the video for the day but these are rare occasions.
